TOKENOMICS

2% Marketing

2% of all transactions will go to our team marketing wallet to ensure we are able to come up with the best campaigns to get a great return on your investment

5%/10% USDT

Automatically earn 5-10% Binance pegged USDT rewards just for holding LION APY . 5% of every buy and 10% every sell is awarded to holders automatically in their wallets.

1% BURN

1% of each transaction is transferred to the burning wallet to create a reliable and untouchable supply of coins that will be replenished and which cannot be sold

2%/3% LP

2% of every buy and 3% of every sale is automatically credited to a locked liquidity wallet in the form of $BNB, providing ongoing liquidity to stabilize prices. The liquidity pool is locked and certified.
